# KidsClaw
KidsClaw is a new platform for parents and their kids to experience OpenClaw together. It's engaging, fun, and educational.  Parents can setup OpenClaw accounts for their kids. Their kids can then use OpenClaw to play educational games. Parents can monitor kids activity for safety purposes.

The KidsClaw brand is kids vibe style, i.e. Nintendo Game brand.

The KidsClaw product experience is mobile first for kids but also desktop friendly for parents who want to manage their family accounts from a laptop. 

Parents can also join other parents in a network of families using KidsClaw, i.e. KidsClaw Club.

## Onboarding Experience
- Parents visit https://kidsclaw.club and create an account.
- Then they click a button to launch their KidsClaw instance.
- In the background a VPS is provisioned on Hetzner and installs an OpenClaw instance pre-configured for KidsClaw. 
- After the VPS and OpenClaw provisioning is complete parents can connect with their KidsClaw instance in the same browser they're logged into KidsClaw with.
- Then parents can add their kids and their mobile devices to their KidsClaw instance. 
- Then kids can start playing KidsClaw games on their smartphones or tablets.
IMPORTANT: The onboarding experience needs to be smooth and error free.  And it needs to be as fast as possible so parents aren't waiting a long time for provisioning.  It should also be very easy for kids to start playing KidsClaw games once they've been added by their parents.  Maybe as simple as a text message, or QR code? 

## Tech Stack
 For the MVP we want to test if Nextjs is the right web application framework, hosted on Vercel.  I'm open to suggestions for other tech stacks to achieve the product experience.
